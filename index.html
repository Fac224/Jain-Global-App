Strategies

Jain Global employs a diversified approach across seven distinct investment strategies
Fundamental Equity – 30% AUM

Focuses on long-term investments in undervalued companies with strong growth potential.

Equity Arbitrage – 20% AUM

Involves exploiting price discrepancies between related equity securities.

Commodities – 13% AUM

Invests in physical goods and derivative contracts to capitalize on market fluctuations.

Credit – 12% AUM

Targets opportunities in corporate and sovereign debt markets.

Rates and Macro – 15% AUM

Centers on interest rate movements and macroeconomic trends to inform investment decisions.

Systematic – 12% AUM

Utilizes quantitative models and algorithms to identify trading opportunities.
